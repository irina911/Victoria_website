About Victoria

Victoria M is a fitness and dance instructor based in South Brooklyn. She offers personal and group classes, including Zumba, Pilates, stretching, step, Latin dance, bachata, hip-hop, ballet, and general fitness training. Victoria also provides nutrition guidance to help clients build healthier habits and feel stronger in everyday life.
